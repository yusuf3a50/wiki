### [Delete cookies on exit](https://support.google.com/chrome/community-guide/245444314/how-to-automatically-clear-browsing-data-when-closing-google-chrome-window-a-step-by-step-guide?hl=en):

#### Plan A: 
- Open [this direct link](chrome://settings/content/siteData) in your Google Chrome web browser to enable this feature, or:


#### Plan B.:
1. Open Chrome Settings from the 3 dots icon, and then choose Settings
2. Click on Privacy and Security
3. Navigate to Site Settings
4. Select Additional Content Settings from the menu
5. Click on On-device Site Data
6. Select now the radio button Delete data sites that have been saved to your device when you close all windows
